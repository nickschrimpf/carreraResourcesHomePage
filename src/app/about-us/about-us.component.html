Our Mission

As a commercial enterprise, Carrera Resources, Inc exists to generate sales and profits for its owners, investors, and business partners through acquisition, development, and revitalization of commercial assets. As a service organization, Carrera Resources, Inc also exists to provide professional, high-value service to its clients by helping them achieve their specific objectives. These two business drivers are inextricable; if one is not successful, the other will fail also.

To accomplish this mission, we will at all times strive for Integrity, Excellence, and Vision in every facet of our business.
Carrera Resources, Inc

Founded in 2007, Carrera Resources, Inc is a privately held Colorado Corporation dedicated to creating broad-based economic value by helping owners, investors, lenders, and communities solve difficult real estate problems.

Our Services

Carrera Resources, Inc has leveraged the education, experience, and skills of the company principals to develop a set of core business competencies upon which we will establish, grow, and continually refine a full range of real estate investment services.

  • We have established and will adhere to an economically, technically, and operationally sustainable business model for the efficient and cost-effective acquisition and repositioning of under-performing and distressed real estate assets.
  • We employ proven project management methodologies that support identification, planning, and integration of activities, efficient allocation of resources, and execution of work in accordance with established requirements.
  • We have established and will adhere to standard, repeatable, and auditable business processes to ensure complete transparency and integrity in all transactions.
  • We have established and will continue to develop and maintain strong, mutually beneficial relationships with a core team of resources including lenders, investors, appraisers, inspectors, contractors, legal counsel, brokers, property managers, mentors, and other professionals
